Clicker Game Information

Welcome to the Clicker Game! This game is a simple yet addictive clicker game where you can earn clicks by clicking a button and use those clicks to purchase upgrades in the shop.

How to Play

  • Click the "click for clicks" button to earn clicks.
  • Use your clicks to buy upgrades in the shop by clicking the "shop" button.
  • Upgrades will help you earn clicks faster!
  • Your highest score will be saved and displayed at the bottom left of the screen.
